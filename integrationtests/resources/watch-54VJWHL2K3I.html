This is a parody song music video of "Like a g6" by Far East Movement. I hope you enjoy it. Also, to make it absolutely clear, this video was made entirely by me. None of this has been stolen from other films or videos, and the music was build by me from scratch using Garage band based off of the "G6" melody. Its like what Weird Al does, but he gets paid.

Dungeons and Dragons belongs to Wizards of the Coast, whom I am in no way involved with. This is purely nerds being nerds having fun and honoring a game that has brought so much wonderfully lost time into our lives.
